Hi, I'm Jessica!

I'm a web developer, ghostwriter, and former secondary school teacher. Technology always played a role in my teaching methods, from learning management systems and research platforms to interactive whiteboards, web quests and wikis.

Since leaving the public education system, my focus has shifted from the user experience side of technology to learning as much as I can about frontend and backend web development and web content writing. And I've continued helping others learn in addition to my volunteer work in the community.
